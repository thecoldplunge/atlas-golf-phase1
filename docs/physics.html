4. Swing + Tempo

Perfect tempo window
85–165 ms
Brief pause at the top. Too short = rushed, too long = frozen.
Rushed penalty
1.55× deviation

Frozen penalty
1.45× deviation
Holding too long at the top also hurts.
Acceleration checks
Decel or yank punished
Too lazy through impact or too violent both reduce control.

5. Overpower Risk

100%
Baseline
Normal risk and deviation.
110%
Noticeably riskier
Tempo strictness and deviation penalties start stacking.
120%
High risk

Overpower curve
Exponential

6. Surfaces / Lies

Fairway
Baseline roll and clean contact.
Rough
More drag and less clean strike.
Sand
Heavy power penalty, weak launch, ugly contact.
Green / Fringe
Roll physics + slope break dominate.

7. Clubs

Every club has a few core values: carryYards, speed, launch, roll, and spin. These define how far it flies, how high it launches, how much it releases, and how much it curves/holds.

8. Best knobs to tune first

If ball flight feels too floaty
Increase GRAVITY or reduce club launch values.
If putts don’t break enough
Increase SLOPE_FORCE.
If tempo feels too easy
Tighten the tempo windows and raise rushed/frozen penalties.
If 120% is too safe
Raise overpower penalty multipliers.
