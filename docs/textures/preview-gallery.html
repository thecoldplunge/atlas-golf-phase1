Palette references: Golf Story fairway #7FB23A–#8BC24C, Sports Story water #4FB8C9–#5FC9D8, tree canopy always 3 shades + 1 dark outline + warm brown trunk. Drop shadows are flat ellipses at ~50% black-green, no directional offset.

Fairway

The lit, mowed corridor. Should read as "hit here, you're safe" at a glance.

AVertical mowing stripes

Current style, tightened. Reads like freshly mowed fairway; strong direction cue.

BDiagonal stripes (Augusta)

Same stripe width, rotated 30°. More prestigious, slightly harder to read on short holes.

CFlat with grass stipple (Golf Story)

No stripes — just a sparse grass-highlight stipple. Cozy, rural, closer to Golf Story. My rec.

Putting Green

Brighter than fairway, subtly different texture. Must read as "this is where you putt."

ADiagonal mowing

Current style, tightened brightness. Classic putting-green look.

BRadial sheen

Center brighter than edges. Gives greens a "lit from above" feel. No pattern.

CTight grass stipple

Small dense highlight dots, no stripes. Cleanest at small scale. Pairs well with Fairway C.

Rough

Missed-fairway territory. Darker, visibly tufty. Golf Story uses a very readable grass-blade motif here.

AGrass blade strokes

Small curved blade strokes every ~20px. Most Golf-Story-accurate rough.

BTuft dots

Soft highlight + shadow dots, no strokes. Reads as rough but more abstract than A.

CCrosshatch

Darker crosshatch suggests tangled grass. Reads well at distance; busy up close.

Deep Rough

The "don't go here" band. Darker than rough, coarser.

ATall blade strokes

Same motif as Rough A, longer strokes, on darker base.

BClumps

Dense dark clumps — shrubby. Good contrast against rough.

CFlat (no texture)

Baseline. Relies on color alone. Cheapest to render, reads cleanest from altitude.

Sand Bunker

Warm tan. Needs to read as soft / loose at a glance.

AGrain stipple

Fine warm-brown dots on pale cream. Matches Golf Story's wheat-colored sand.

BInset shadow

Bright center fading to a darker rim — reads as a depression. Common for bunkers.

CStipple + crisp rim (Golf Story)

Cream fill, fine grain dots, and a crisp 2px darker-tan inner rim. Sharpest bunker read. My rec.

Water

Should stop the eye. Layered blues, subtle ripples.

AGradient + soft waves

Cyan-turquoise gradient with curvy highlight lines. Reads as gentle rippling water.

BBroken-line ripples (Golf Story)

Cyan base with dashed horizontal highlight segments. The exact Golf Story / Sports Story water treatment. My rec.

CSparkle dots

Scattered light + dark dots. Busier, more "pond" less "ocean." Works at close zoom.
