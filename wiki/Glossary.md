# Glossary

Quick reference for terms used across the wiki and codebase.

---

## Organizations & places

- **IGT — Intergalactic Golf Tour.** The most prestigious sporting organization in known space. Chartered 2155 under the Neutral Compact of Aeris Station. Governs standardized rules, course certification, and unified rankings across fourteen signatory species.
- **Neutral Compact of Aeris Station.** The fragile treaty that governs the IGT. Signed by fourteen species.
- **Aeris Station.** The IGT's administrative hub in deep space. Home of the **Aeris Open** Major.
- **Earth Tour.** Earth's top-level professional circuit. Feeder to the IGT via international invitations.
- **Open tournaments.** Pay-to-enter amateur events. The bottom rung of the career ladder.

---

## The Majors (Grand Slam)

- **The Aeris Open** — on Aeris Station.
- **The Keldaran Masters** — on Keldara-7, Voss homeworld.
- **The Rill Invitational** — on a Rill Consortium ice shelf.
- **The Sol Classic** — on Earth. The one every human wants to win.

Holding all four is the **Grand Slam**. No human has ever done it.

---

## Technology

- **Tanaka Fold Drive.** The space-folding propulsion system invented by Dr. Yuna Tanaka (patent 2103). Collapses the distance between two points; doesn't move ships faster than light — moves the space. Travel cost is the foundation of the game's economy.
- **Helion-9.** Synthetic fuel refined from gas-giant atmospheres. Powers fold jumps. Expensive.
- **Fold-jump / fold.** A single interstellar journey via the Tanaka Drive. Each jump costs credits.
- **Fold-relay network.** How IGT broadcasts reach every signatory world.

---

## Species

- **Human.** Generalists. No standout advantage, no crippling weakness. "Unusual capacity for clutch performance under pressure." Underdogs. You.
- **Voss (Voss Hegemony).** Tall, pressure-adapted, dense musculature. Power archetype. Homeworld: Keldara-7.
- **Rill (Rill Consortium).** Near-perfect spatial calculation, low raw power. Precision archetype. Play their native variant on frictionless ice shelves.
- **Paxi (Paxi Collective).** Biosensory wind-reading. Touch / feel archetype.
- Plus eleven other signatory species across the Orion Arm.

## Stats

Each golfer has nine stats, 0–100:

- **Power** — clubhead speed / carry distance ceiling.
- **Accuracy** — how tightly shots cluster around the intended line.
- **Touch** — feel on short/finesse shots around the green.
- **Spin Control** — how cleanly a player can apply and shape spin.
- **Putting** — green reading + putt execution.
- **Composure** — resistance to tilt after bad breaks.
- **Course Management** — quality of strategic decisions.
- **Focus** — moment-to-moment concentration; degraded by lodging conditions.
- **Recovery** — escape / scramble shots from trouble.

## Economy

- **Credits.** The universal currency. Fold-jumps, lodging, food, entry fees, gear, training — all denominated in credits.
- **Purse.** Total prize pool of a tournament, distributed by finish position.
- **Cut.** Finishing threshold mid-tournament. Miss it and you don't play the final rounds — and you don't cash.
- **Appearance fee.** What an apparel sponsor pays you per tournament just to show up in their kit.
- **Rental.** How equipment sponsors give you clubs — you use them as long as you hold the ranking; they get them back when you drop.

---

## Game systems

- **Back lot.** The unofficial side-bet zone behind every pro shop. Skins, Nassau, scramble, parking-lot exhibitions.
- **Caddie hall.** Where broke players pick up caddie gigs. Core loop of the broke-recovery mini-game.
- **Sponsor politics.** The meta-game of which sponsors will sign you given your existing contracts, rivalries, and media obligations.
- **Tour Card.** Your credential to play a given tour (Earth Tour Card, IGT Tour Card). Earned by finish position; revocable by ranking drop.
