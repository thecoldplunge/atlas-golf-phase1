## The one-line vision

**You start broke on your parents' couch on Earth. You finish hoisting the Sol Classic as the first human in history to hold all four Majors.** Every credit in between, you earned.

## The three pillars

1. **Golf as survival.** The money is the game — lodging, food, fold-jumps, entry fees, gear repair, training all bleed from your account every week.
2. **Every stat costs money.** Power, Accuracy, Touch, Spin Control, Putting, Composure, Course Management — improved through paid training with mini-games.
3. **Underdog fantasy.** Humans are generalists playing against species that have been at this longer than our civilization has had fold tech.

## Contributing to this wiki

- The **Current State** page must be updated whenever a feature ships. Keep it honest — if it's not in the build, don't list it.
- The **Grand Vision** page is the north star. Change it only on an explicit design decision, not based on what ended up easier to build.
- New systems get their own subpage once they're non-trivial (e.g. `Economy`, `Sponsorships`, `Caddie-Mini-Game`). Link them from Home and Grand Vision.
- Keep lore in **Backstory**. Keep mechanics in **Grand Vision**. Keep shipped reality in **Current State**.
